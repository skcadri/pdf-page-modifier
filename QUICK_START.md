# 🚀 Quick Start Guide

**For Users Who Just Want to Modify PDFs**

## 📥 **Step 1: Get the App**
1. Download this repository (click the green "Code" button → "Download ZIP")
2. Extract the ZIP file to your computer
3. Find the file named `PDF_Page_Modifier.exe`

## 🖱️ **Step 2: Run the App**
1. **Double-click** `PDF_Page_Modifier.exe`
2. The application will open (no installation needed!)

## 📄 **Step 3: Load Your PDF**
1. Click **"Load PDF File"**
2. Select your PDF from your computer
3. Wait for page thumbnails to appear

## 🎯 **Step 4: Choose What to Do**
**Two modes available:**

### 🗑️ **Remove Unwanted Pages**
1. Keep **"Remove Selected Pages"** selected (default)
2. **Click on pages** you want to DELETE (they turn red)
3. Click **"Save Modified PDF"**
4. Your new PDF will have those pages removed!

### 📎 **Extract Specific Pages**  
1. Select **"Keep Selected Pages"** 
2. **Click on pages** you want to KEEP (they turn green)
3. Click **"Save Modified PDF"**
4. Your new PDF will have only those pages!

## 💾 **Step 5: Save Your Work**
1. Choose where to save your new PDF
2. Give it a name
3. Click **"📂 Open PDF"** to view it immediately!

## 🎉 **That's It!**
Your modified PDF is ready to use!

---

## 💡 **Quick Tips**
- **Red borders** = Pages will be removed
- **Green borders** = Pages will be kept  
- **"Select All"** button selects all pages at once
- **"Clear Selection"** removes all selections
- **No internet required** - works completely offline!
